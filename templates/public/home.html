Est. 1935

A Legacy 91 Years in the Making

Dulalpur Chandramoni High School has been the cornerstone of education in the Dulalpur community since 1935. Generations of students have walked through its halls, gone on to build careers, and returned to give back — creating the living legacy that is the Chandramonians community.

The alumni association was founded to keep this tradition alive — to ensure no student is left behind, and no alumnus loses their connection to the school that shaped them.
