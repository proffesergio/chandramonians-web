The official alumni association of Dulalpur Chandramoni High School — connecting generations of graduates, supporting current students, and preserving the legacy of our institution.

Our Mission

To unite alumni of Dulalpur Chandramoni High School across all generations, foster a culture of giving back, and create meaningful opportunities for current students through mentorship, scholarships, and community building.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the most active alumni association in the region — a network where every DCMHS graduate feels proud, connected, and empowered to contribute to the school's growth and the advancement of education in our community.
